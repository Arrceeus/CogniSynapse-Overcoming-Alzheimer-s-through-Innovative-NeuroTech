Home

Hello, it's me, Rafi. The web app is still in its early version, and it's been a while since I last updated it. I abandoned this project in 2024 because I was busy with life, but I plan to create a better version in the future.

This free webapp solution saves your time and money and it uses advanced technology to detect early symptoms of Alzheimer's disease. By analyzing data from cognitive tests and tracking changes in behavior and memory. This app can provide early detection and alert users to seek medical attention.

It's an All in One App with a tons of features!

Innovative technology is used here to improve the lives of those with Alzheimer's. Virtual reality and artificial intelligence are just two examples of how technology is being used here to unlock memories and provide a better quality of life.

  • Medication Tracker

    Cognition Assessment Test!

    Verbal Reasoning:

    Which word does not belong in the following group and why?

    a) Apple
    b) Banana
    c) Orange
    d) Carrot

    Numerical Reasoning:

    If a train travels 60 miles in 1 hour, how far will it travel in 3 hours at the same speed?

    a) 10
    b) 20
    c) 90
    d) 35

    Logical Testing:

    If all birds can fly, and a penguin is a bird, can a penguin fly?

    a) Yes
    b) No

    Memory Recall:

    Study the following list of words for 30 seconds, then write down as many as you can remember: Apple, Chair, Butterfly, Elephant, Lamp, Guitar, Orange, Tiger, Banana, Clock

    Pattern Recognition Test:

    For each word provided, try to think of another word that is closely associated with it.

    Sun, Cake, Blue, Guitar, Rain

    Hint / Example:

    Sun → Sky , Cake → Celebration , Blue → Color, Guitar → Music, Rain → Umbrella
